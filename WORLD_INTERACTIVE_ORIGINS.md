## East Flatbush Origins — Priority World

This world places the student inside East Flatbush, Brooklyn in the 1990s.
Every building, every face, every sound is drawn from community-contributed
oral histories and archival photos.

### What a Student Experiences
1. **Arrival** — Spawn at the corner of E 94th St and Flatbush Ave, 1993
2. **Exploration** — Walk the block; enter shops, hear music, meet AI residents
3. **The 458 Encounter** — Enter 458 E 94th St; the building tells its own story
4. **The Frequency Quest** — Unlock musical, cultural, and historical knowledge
5. **Community Ledger** — See how the block's economy worked; earn EBTK tokens

### Connected Repos
- `roblox-wealthbridge-east-flatbush`: Roblox game layer, EBTK economy
- `eastflatbush-90s-unity`: Unity 3D world build
- `nvidia-resource-suite`: Omniverse scene + NIM tutor
- `synapz`: Documentary distribution (The 458 Frequency)
- `synapz-social`: Community platform for alumni and contributors
